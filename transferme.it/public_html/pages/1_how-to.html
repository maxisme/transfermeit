Send a file?

There are three different ways to simply and efficiently send your file!

Drag a file from Finder onto the menu bar icon.

 

OR

 

Right Click a file in Finder and choose Send File (or: Services > Send File).

 

OR

 

Click on the menu bar icon then click Send File and choose your file.

After choosing the file. You then must enter your friends code and press enter or click the paper plane.

Create a code?

By default transfer me it will create a 10 minute code to identify you. This means a friend can only send a file to you within 10 minutes and the server will also only store your encrypted file on the server for a maximum of 10 (inactive) minutes - Files are immediately deleted once your friend has downloaded the file. Depending on your credit you can choose up to 1 hour.
